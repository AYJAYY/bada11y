Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What fitness level is required? +
We offer adventures for all fitness levels. Each package carries a difficulty rating from 1–5. We recommend consulting a physician before any high-altitude trekking above 4,000m.
What is included in the price? +
All packages include accommodation, all meals from Day 1 dinner, experienced guides, park permits, and airport transfers. International flights are not included.
Is travel insurance required? +
Yes. Comprehensive travel and emergency medical evacuation insurance is mandatory for all participants. We recommend specialist adventure travel policies.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations 90+ days before departure: full refund minus deposit. 60–89 days: 50% refund. Fewer than 60 days: no refund. Travel insurance is strongly recommended.

All departure dates are subject to minimum group numbers being reached. Summit Adventures will notify you no later than 60 days before departure if a trip is cancelled due to insufficient bookings.
